# 🎬 OTT vs Cinema Research Project

## Research Objective

A research project designed to understand changing consumer behavior and preferences between OTT platforms and cinema experiences.

### Research Methodology

✓ Descriptive & Comparative Quantitative Research

✓ Cross-sectional study design

✓ Primary + Secondary Research

✓ Survey-based analysis

### Data Collection

• 85 Respondents

• Google Forms Survey

• Snowball Sampling

• Multiple demographic segments

### Research Scope

• Consumer preference analysis

• Pricing behavior analysis

• OTT vs Cinema comparison

• Loyalty & switching factors

• Consumer experience evaluation

### Key Findings

📊 Cinema Preference:

49.38%

📊 OTT Preference:

41.98%

📊 OTT Users:

70.9%

📊 Most Cinema Loyal Segment:

Age 25–34

### Strategic Insights

✓ Consumer behavior is shifting toward hybrid consumption

✓ Convenience drives OTT growth

✓ Cinema remains strong for immersive experiences

✓ Future opportunities exist through OTT + Cinema synergy

`Consumer Behavior Analysis`

`Survey Analysis`

`Business Research`
